What can you do with Shareist?

  • Are you a Writer?

    You are writing a short ebook. As part of your research, you surf the web and add relevant content, images, and videos to your Shareist Notebook. You edit your pages by adding your own copy and images, customize and categorize. When you’re finished, Shareist makes it easy to turn it into a great looking eBook.

  • Are you a Blogger?

    Coming up with new ideas can be challenging. Shareist helps you capture ideas when you have them and inspiring posts from the web when you see them. The Shareist Content Stream helps you keep your eye on the latest news, videos, tweets on your subject. Work on your posts in private, and when they’re ready, publish them on your Notebook or export them to your Wordpress blog.

  • Are you a Teacher?

    You teach History. You have several Shareist Notebooks, one for each of your classes. You include links to information, videos, and images that you trust, your own perspective and insights. You can order and then re-order the information to follow your class curriculum. Your students can access your Notebooks at anytime online.

  • Are you a MOM?

    You share stuff on Facebook, but sometimes it feels like you’re sharing too much. Your Shareist notebook is your personal place to capture everything and anything: Stories, pictures, and videos of your kids to share with your family and friends. Jot down stuff you want to buy, or read later. Keep some stuff private, and other stuff public. Share just the best stuff to Facebook and your blog.
